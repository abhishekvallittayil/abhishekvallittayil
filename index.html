// bio

My journey into cybersecurity began in 2019 during a college workshop. During COVID-19 lockdown in 2020, I taught myself Linux and explored cybersecurity. Since then I earned CEH, Security+, and CPT certifications and gained hands-on experience with Windows Server, Active Directory, VPNs, and network security through my Cybersecurity Capstone Project.

I also created LiveLongX, a health-focused web platform, and am working on a DeepStride health band project. I am preparing for CCNA and seeking opportunities across Canada.

My Windows Server enterprise network with VPNs, firewalls, and vulnerability assessments — and LiveLongX, my health platform.
